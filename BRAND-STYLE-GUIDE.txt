BRAND IDENTITY
====================================================

BRAND NAME: Unscarred
STYLIZED: un<scarred> (with "scarred" in primary rose color)

TAGLINE: 
"Pattern support for the ones who feel too much and see too clearly."

FOUNDER: Scar Kade

VOICE: 
- Direct, not clinical
- Compassionate, not soft
- Real, not performative
- Educational, not therapeutic
- No shame, just clarity

POSITIONING:
"I'm not a therapist. I'm a survivor, pattern reader, and educator.
Pattern tracking kept me alive. Now I teach it.
Your patterns are not your fault. Learning them is your power."

====================================================
WRITING STYLE
====================================================

HEADLINES:
- Short, direct, evocative
- Use "you/your" language
- Examples:
  "Break The Bond"
  "Anxious Healing"
  "Coming Home"

DESCRIPTIONS:
- Start with the pain point (you...)
- Explain what this solves
- List what's included (bullets)
- End with who it's for
- No clinical jargon
- No shame language

SIGNATURE PHRASES:
- "Your patterns are not your fault. Learning them is your power."
- "I turn scars into mirrors."
- "This isn't about fixing you. It's about understanding you."
- "No shame. Just clarity."
- "For the ones who feel too much and see too clearly."
