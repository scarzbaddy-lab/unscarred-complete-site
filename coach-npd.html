Common questions

Do I need a diagnosis to work with you?
No. Many people have narcissistic patterns without meeting clinical criteria for NPD. If you recognize yourself in the patterns described above, this can help regardless of diagnosis.
Will you tell me I'm a narcissist?
I'm not here to diagnose you. I'm here to help you see your patterns clearly and build different responses. Whether you use the NPD label is up to you.
Is this going to be you attacking me for an hour?
No. Direct doesn't mean aggressive. I'll reflect what I see honestly, but the goal is clarity and tools, not shame. If you're expecting someone to beat you up, you'll be surprised.
What if my partner is the one who thinks I'm narcissistic?
We can work with that. Sometimes it's accurate, sometimes it's projection, often it's a mix. We'll look at the actual patterns, not the labels.
