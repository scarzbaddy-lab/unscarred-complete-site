Parentified Child Check

Did you raise your parents? Map the role reversal and its echoes.

Parentification happens when a child takes on adult responsibilities that should have been carried by caregivers. It robs children of their childhood and creates patterns that echo into adult relationships.

This assessment identifies signs of parentification and how they may be showing up in your life now.
