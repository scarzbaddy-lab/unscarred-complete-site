Are you a therapist?
Nope. I'm Scar. Trauma-informed educator, pattern reader, and survivor. I don't diagnose, treat, or provide clinical care. What I offer is pattern support: helping you understand your attachment style, nervous system responses, and relationship dynamics. It's the curriculum nobody taught you.
What if I'm in crisis right now?
If you're in immediate danger or having thoughts of harming yourself, please contact emergency services or a crisis line first. My work is meant to supplement professional care, not replace it. Once you're stable, I can help you understand the patterns that got you there.
Which product should I start with?
Start with a quiz to map your patterns. If you're anxious-attached, try the Regulation Workbook. If you're in a complicated relationship dynamic, the Pattern Tracking session gives you a full map. If you need daily support, Best Friend Mode is the way to go.
How is this different from therapy?
Therapy treats clinical conditions and processes deep trauma with professional protocols. I focus on pattern education: helping you understand WHY you react the way you do and giving you practical scripts and tools to respond differently. Many of my clients use my work alongside therapy.
Do you offer refunds?
Digital products are non-refundable once accessed. For 1:1 calls, I offer rescheduling with 24-hour notice. If something doesn't feel right, reach out and we'll figure it out together.
Can I book a call to ask questions first?
Yes! I offer a free 15-minute consult if you're not sure which service fits. DM me or hit the "Book a Call" button and we'll figure out what makes sense for where you are.
