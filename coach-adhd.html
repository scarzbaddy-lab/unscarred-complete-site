Common questions

Do I need an ADHD diagnosis?
No. If you recognize yourself in the patterns above (the task paralysis, the rejection sensitivity, the time blindness), this can help whether or not you have a formal diagnosis. Many adults are undiagnosed or self-diagnosed.
What is body doubling exactly?
Body doubling is having another person present (physically or virtually) while you work. For ADHD brains, this external presence often makes task initiation much easier. You don't have to talk or interact—just knowing someone is there creates enough structure to start.
How is this different from regular ADHD coaching?
I focus specifically on relational patterns and emotional regulation alongside executive function. If ADHD is affecting your relationships—your partner thinks you don't care, you can't manage shared responsibilities, RSD is destroying your connections—that's what we work on together.
What if I need medication management?
That's outside my scope—you'll need a psychiatrist or prescriber for medication. I can help you build habits and structures that work with or without medication, and help you track what's working to share with your prescriber.
I've tried everything and nothing works
You've probably tried systems designed for neurotypical brains. We'll build something designed for yours—starting small, building on dopamine, and creating external scaffolding that gradually becomes internal habit.
