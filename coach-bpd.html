Common questions

Do I need a BPD diagnosis?
No. If you recognize yourself in the patterns above (the splitting, the abandonment alarm, the intensity), this can help whether or not you have a formal diagnosis. Many people have these patterns without meeting full criteria.
Is this a replacement for DBT?
No. If you're in DBT, keep going. This is complementary, pattern coaching focused on relationships and real-time situations. It's less structured than DBT but more personalized to your specific patterns.
What does "text me before you text them" mean?
Bundle clients get access to text me when they're activated, before they send the spiral text to their person. I'll help you check the alarm, translate the emotion, and send something that doesn't blow up the relationship.
What if I'm in a relationship with someone with BPD?
That's a different service. Check out the Translation Sessions on the coaching page. This page is specifically for people working on their own BPD patterns.
I'm scared you'll judge me for what I've done
I've seen it all and done half of it myself. This isn't a space for judgment. It's a space for understanding why you did the thing and building something different. Shame doesn't change patterns; clarity does.
