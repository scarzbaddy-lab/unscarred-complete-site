====================================================
QUICK CANVA SETUP TEMPLATE
====================================================

1. Create new design: 1600 x 900px
2. Background: #050507 (solid)
3. Add rectangle overlay: 
   - Gradient radial, #a33b5c at 0%, transparent at 60%
   - Position: bottom-left corner
   - Opacity: 30%
4. Add icon: centered-left or center
5. Add headline: Cormorant Garamond, Italic, 600, #ece8e1
6. Add subtext: Outfit, 400, uppercase, #9a958c
7. Add price/badge if applicable
8. Export as PNG
